📘 How the Game Works

🔹 1. Difficulty Levels

Each difficulty level controls how tilted the animal appears:

  • Easy: -45° (Very obvious direction)
  • Medium: -22.5° (Moderate tilt)
  • Difficult: -9.5° (Slight tilt)
  • Adaptive: Adjusts dynamically as the player performs better or worse

🔹 2. How Animals Move

Animals move horizontally across the screen, but their container is rotated, creating a diagonal movement illusion.

  • Direction = Q: Left
  • Direction = P: Right

🔹 3. What Controls Difficulty

Difficulty dynamically updates based on how many responses were correct or incorrect in a row (2 correct increases difficulty, 2 wrong decreases it).

🔹 4. Key Mechanics

  • ang: Rotation of the animal container
  • x: Array of positions for the animal
  • barWidth: Width of the animal image
  • direction: 1 for left, -1 for right

🔹 5. What You Experience

  1. Animal slides diagonally
  2. Guess direction with P or Q
  3. Feedback is given instantly
  4. Chart at the end shows your performance
